# We are artists

<Date date="2025-03-31T17:15:41.104Z" />


Entrepreneurs are artists. No doubt about it.

But you don’t need a business or a product to claim that title.
You’re already running something—your life.
You manage your time, your thoughts, your body.
You improvise. You adapt. You build.

So yeah. You’re an artist too.

![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!_6Rk!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F12a3f554-e7aa-4da2-9514-8dbb5e34bf6e_1536x1024.png) An abstract painting gradually blending into a city street scene, where people are walking with intention—each person lit by a soft halo suggesting they are creators of their own paths

Artists make things that didn’t exist before.
That’s the job.

And if you’re going to make something—make it beautiful.
Not perfect. But beautiful in the way it was *meant*to be.

That’s the game.
You dream something up.
You refine it.
You try to make it better.
And even though perfection’s a myth, the act of reaching for it?
That’s real. That’s the art.

![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!FRAu!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F19bba898-3b65-43b7-8e67-8e0fd9da9747_1536x1024.png) Hands sculpting clay on a pottery wheel, focused and fluid, with soft lighting highlighting the detail of the process

Lately, I’ve been thinking about execution.

Ideas are nothing without motion.
You can sit with a vision for years and never take a single step.
But execution—that’s what changes things.
That’s what makes artists artists.

They move.

Waking up? That’s your first execution.
You showed up. You stepped into the day.

But you can go further.
Raise the bar.
Do more with what you’ve got.

Because we’re not here to wait.
We’re here to live.

![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!pMrF!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F3a73fb5b-4833-4924-b5c6-47b8da7008f9_1536x1024.png) Someone standing at sunrise on a quiet street, stretching and breathing in the morning air, with a subtle overlay of blooming flowers suggesting growth and potential

So go find what’s worth loving.
What brings you joy.
What makes you pause just to appreciate the beauty of it.

That’s the art. That’s the point.
